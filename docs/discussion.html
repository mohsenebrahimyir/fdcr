Figure 8: Preliminary map of the evidence and future directions in biomarker development. The Sankey diagram presents a summary of the methodological parameters and contexts of use (COUs) across the 432 potential biomarkers in the systematic review. Moving forward, expert consensus and meta- and mega-analyses may be used to facilitate harmonization and the development of optimal FDCR biomarkers which would undergo analytical and clinical validation and cost-benefit analysis before regulatory qualification for drug development or clinical use.
